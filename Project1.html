About Me

I'm the contemplative, creative type. I'm a hoarder of ideas. I like to take the complex ones, break them down to their core, and build them up into something practical. I believe that we are living in an age where our world is transforming- faster, smarter - every day. It's an information revolution. And I'm glad to take part in that movement.

I take a particular fascination in interactive applications. Web/software development has become my latest hobbies. Most of my professional experiences has been in financial services. As such, much of my experience was building efficient solutions to database management supporting financial institutions.

I'm currently focused on maximizing my exposure to web and software development whether it requires close team interaction, or virtual projects with little supervisory oversight.
